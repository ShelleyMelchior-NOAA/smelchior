NOTES:


(1)   The closed bracket sign ) indicates that the corresponding code figure is reserved for the previously named center.


(2)    To indicate whether the originating/generating center is a sub-center or not, the following procedure should be applied:
         In BUFR Edition 3, use octet 5 of section 1 with the following meaning:
         Code figure
         0                  Not a sub-center, the originating/generating center is the center defined by octet 6 in section 1 of BUFR edition 3.
         1 to 254       Identifier of the sub center which is the originating/generating center. The identifier of the sub-center is allocated by the associated center, which is defined by octet 6

                             in section 1 of BUFR edition 3. The sub-center identifiers should be supplied to the WMO Secretariat by the associated center(s) for publication.     

   

(3)    For the definitions of sub-centers definition provided to the WMO Secretariat, see Common table C-12.
